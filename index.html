About New golden decorator & caterer

Transforming dreams into timeless memories since 2016

New golden decorator & caterer is a premier wedding planning and design firm based in New Delhi, India. We specialize in creating bespoke weddings that reflect your unique story. From intimate gatherings to grand destination celebrations, our passionate team ensures every moment is flawlessly executed.

With a blend of creativity, meticulous planning, and a network of the finest vendors, we handle everything – venue selection, décor conceptualization, hospitality, entertainment, and more – so you can relax and cherish every moment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer? +
We offer end-to-end wedding planning including design & décor, entertainment, destination wedding logistics, vendor management, hospitality, and budget planning. We also provide partial planning and day-of coordination.
Do you plan destination weddings? +
Absolutely! We specialize in destination weddings across India and abroad. Our team handles travel arrangements, accommodation, legal requirements, and local vendor liaison.
How do you charge for your services? +
Our fees are typically a percentage of the overall wedding budget, but we also offer fixed-fee packages for smaller events. Contact us for a personalized quote.
Can we hire you for only specific services? +
Yes, we offer custom packages where you can pick and choose services like décor only, vendor referrals, or on-the-day coordination.
How far in advance should we book? +
We recommend booking at least 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding seasons. However, we also accommodate last-minute requests based on availability.
